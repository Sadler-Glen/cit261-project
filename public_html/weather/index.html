About

This single paged APP has been created as part of a personal project for CIT-261: JavaScript Mobile Software Development.

This course has been designed to allow us to become more professional by learning in an environment where the instructor is our mentor rather than a lecturer. Using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript we learned to explore technologies in order to resolve problems that would arise naturally. We also learned to find, evaluate, and select solutions to those same problems through creating this single page mobile web application. Having had such a mentored learning experience is very important as we begin our careers. Exploration of tech to solve previously unsolved problems is what we will be doing for our job.

We worked in a group but we were responsible for our own learning. This was accomplished when our learning, not the team's, was evaluated. To accomplish this, assessment points were created in the course. These consisted of providing evidence of our fluency in the technologies in the form of sandbox code, and code we, not our team, have written. Additional evidence of professionalism is also a requirement. We came to see what these were and how our fluency and professionalism was to be assessed by viewing the course rubric.
